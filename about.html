Our Story:

SivaParvathi Rice Mill is Started by Cherukuri Devarajulu at 2014 and Still Successfully Running and 24/7 it works.
With over 500 farmer members, a leading rice mill in India, SivaParvathi ships products in foodservice, retail, export, and ingredients markets.
From the land to the customer, Siva Parvathi is totally integrated as a health-centric mill offering the most efficient quality product, Direct from the grower to the customer.
SivaParvathi Mill is located in the heart of South India, Andhra Pradesh, the leading rice-producing state in the country.
Our growers have been part of the ShivaParvathi family. Though we primarily operate in Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu. We value the efforts of our farmers or “rice producers,” and the care they give to the rice crops and land, thus the name we proudly share,SivaParvathi Rice Mill.
